Publish as ePUB

Review the information here before you do the steps in Save ePUB file.

  • At any time, click PREVIEW to see a preview of your book.

The ePUB window has two panes:

  • Left pane — Here you see the preview of your book in a simulated phone.

  • The right arrow or left arrow display the next or previous page.

  • Refresh updates the preview.

  • The SAVE button lets you choose a folder where you want to save your ePUB.

  •  Notes with information and links to additional information.

  • Right pane — Here you see these controls:

  • ePUB mode chooser. See: ePUB.

  • Accessibility check box. See: Accessibility.

  • A link that opens the Bloom Accessibility Checker dialog box.

  • A link that opens the Book Metadata dialog box.

  • Help has links that open topics in this Bloom Help system or to information on the Internet.

Tip

  • https://docs.bloomlibrary.org/ePUB-notes has information about the performance of various ePUB readers.

  • Recorded audio of the words in the book or of the image descriptions can be part of the saved file.
