Making BloomPUB Apps with Reading App Builder

 BloomPUB is one of the digital publishing options. 

Where to begin:

  • https://software.sil.org/readingappbuilder/download/

From this Internet site, you can download the program, and then install it. 

  • https://software.sil.org/readingappbuilder/resources/

Here you find documentation, development kits and other resources so you can start building reading apps.

Then, you can publish your book as a BloomPUB and make it available to the app.
