Publish your BloomPUB

Do the steps in Viewing BloomPUBs on Bloom Reader (Android).

Then, do these steps:

  1. At any time, click PREVIEW to see a preview of your book.

  2. Text Languages: Select () the languages you want to publish.

    • Clear () the languages you do not want published.

The languages that appear here are those chosen in the Settings dialog box and also others that appear in the source text boxes. In some books you cannot change a selection ().

  1. Talking Book Languages: Select () the languages with audio that you want to publish.

    • Clear () the languages you do not want published.

  2. Features: See About Book Features.

If you select () Motion Book, the portrait and landscape options appears for your preview. Click landscape to see motion in the preview. In portrait, you can verify other things like the text highlighting in a talking book.

  1. Click the button that you see:

  • SAVE

In the Save As dialog box, open the folder where you want to save the BloomPUB (*.bloompub) file, and then click Save.

  • SHARE

Click STOP PUBLISHING in the Publishing dialog box when your book has been sent.

  • CONNECT WITH USB CABLE.

Click STOP PUBLISHING in the Publishing dialog box when your book has been sent.

Tip

  • Optionally, click Make All BloomPUBs from Collection. A Bloom Enterprise Subscription is required.

  • The Thumbnail control is not for use with comics.

  • Below Help, you can

    • Click a link to open a Help topic or a separate file that was installed with Bloom.

    • Click Get Bloom Reader App to open the Google Play store.

  • COPY TO CLIPBOARD in the Creating Digital Book dialog box or Publishing dialog box copies the contents of the dialog box to your clipboard. It can be helpful when you need to get more help.
