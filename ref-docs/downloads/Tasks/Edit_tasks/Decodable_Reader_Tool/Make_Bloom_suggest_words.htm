Make Bloom suggest words for a decodable reader

Bloom will suggest words (sample words or allowed words) after you set up the Decodable Reader Tool.

  • If We are using lists of allowed words to define stages is selected, here are the criteria:

Each stage is linked to an allowed words file.

  • If We are using letters with sight works to define stages is selected, here are the criteria:

    • You have added letters in the Letters tab.

    • You have added and configured at least one stage.

    • You have added sample words in at least one of these places:

New Sight Words box

More Words box

Sample Texts folder (in files).

Tip

Sample words or allowed words are added (cumulatively) up to the current stage. They appear in these places (labels reflect which control is selected):

  • In the Decodable Stages tab as matching words or allowed words.

  • In the Decodable Reader Tool pane, words appear below Sample words in this stage or Allowed words in this stage.
