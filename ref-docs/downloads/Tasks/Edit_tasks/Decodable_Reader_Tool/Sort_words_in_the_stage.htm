Sort words in the current stage

  1. In the Decodable Reader Tool, choose a stage.

Below Words in this stage is a list of words. These words that are in sample text files and use the letters specified up to and including the current stage.

  1. Click one of the these buttons to sort the display of the words:

    • Sort alphabetically

    • Sort by word length

    • Sort by frequency (not available for Allowed words).
