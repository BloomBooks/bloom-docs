Remove a page

You can remove inside pages, except the Title page, from books. You cannot remove pages from calendars. 

  1. Click Collections tab.

  2. In the left pane, click the book or shell book.

  3. Do one of these steps:

    • Double-click the book.

    • Click the Edit tab.

    • Click EDIT THIS BOOK.

  1. In the Pages (left) pane, click the page you want to remove.

  2. Do one of these steps:

    • Right-click the page you want to remove, and then click Remove Page.

    • Click Remove Page on the down arrow menu command.

    • At the bottom of the Pages pane, click Remove this page from the book.

The Really Remove Page question box appears.

  1. To remove the page permanently, click Remove. Otherwise, click Cancel.
