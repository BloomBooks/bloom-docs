Delete a video

If you use the features in the Sign Language Tool to record a video or import one, you can delete it if it is not satisfactory.

  1. In the Pages pane, click the page that has the video you want to delete

  2. Click Advanced.

  3. Click Delete, and then click Delete to confirm the deletion and move the video to your Recycle Bin.
