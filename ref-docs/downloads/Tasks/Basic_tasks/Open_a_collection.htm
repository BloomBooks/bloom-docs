Open a collection

To open a collection that is on your computer, do these steps:

  1. Click the Collections tab.

  2. Click Other Collection on the toolbar.

Enter the password, if you are prompted.

The Open/Create Collections dialog box opens.

  1. If you see the collection you want, click <name>.

  2. If you do not see the collection, click Browse for another collection on this computer.

    • In the Open Collection dialog box, find the folder for the collection.

    • Double-click it to display the <name>.bloomCollection file.

    • Click that file, and then click Open.

The collection opens in the Bloom window.

Tip

  • In the Open/Create Collections dialog box, hold your mouse pointer over the name of a collection to see its path. Then, if you browse for another collection, its folder might be nearby.

  • See Also: Copy a collection to another computer.
