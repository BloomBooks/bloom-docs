Delete a book from BloomLibrary.org

BloomLibrary.org has books that you and others have published.

You can delete books that you uploaded.

Prerequisites

  • You need to have an Internet connection.

  • You need to have an account.

Log in to https://bloomlibrary.org/read and use the controls there to delete your book.

Tip

  • You can update your book in Bloom and upload it again to replace it with a newer version.
