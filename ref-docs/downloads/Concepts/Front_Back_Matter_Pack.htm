Front/Back Matter Pack

In the Book Making tab (Settings dialog box), you can use the Front/Back Matter Pack control to choose a pack.

When you click a pack that was installed with Bloom, a brief explanation appears. It describes the purpose of the pack.

Note

  • If your organization requires a custom Front/Back Matter Pack, SIL can provide that to you through our Bloom Enterprise service.
