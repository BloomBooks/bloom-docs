Sources For New Books

To see Sources For New Books, open a collection.

You see these sources on the Collections tab:

Templates or Specialized Templates

  • These templates were installed with Bloom.

See: Collection tab tasks overview.

Sample Shells

  • These shell books were installed with Bloom.

See: Make a book from a sample shell.

Books From BloomLibrary.org

  • Here you see books that were downloaded. You can make a book from a downloaded book.

  • Click GET MORE SOURCE BOOKS AT BLOOMLIBRARY.ORG to open https://bloomlibrary.org/.

Now you can get books that other people uploaded.

  • Click SHOW ANOTHER COLLECTION to open another collection that is on your computer.
