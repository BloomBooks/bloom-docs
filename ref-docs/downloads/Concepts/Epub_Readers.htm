ePUB Readers

An ePUB reader lets you read a book that was published as an ePUB on electronic devices, such as smart-phones, tablets, computers, and so on.

  • See https://docs.bloomlibrary.org/ePUB-notes to learn about the performance of various ePUB readers.

Both fixed and flowable ePUB modes are considered there. 

Tip

  • You may learn from the community which ePUB readers work best in various situations and the kinds of books you publish.
