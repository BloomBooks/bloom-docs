Collection

In Bloom, a collection is a group of books. 

Essentially, each Bloom book is a little database of texts in various translations, ready to be displayed either in the prompting box, or in the text itself. An interesting implication of this design is that any local language book can become a shell book for other languages; the formats of a shell book and local language book are identical.

Important

  • If you cannot find your book, use the toolbar to open another collection.

  • The Bloom folder is installed here:

Windows® - My Documents\Bloom or Documents\Bloom

Linux - /home/<user name>/ Bloom

  • To find a particular book, in the Collections tab, right-click a book (or a template or sample shell) and then click Show in File Explorer.

  • To learn where a collection is, in the Open/Create Collections dialog box, hold your mouse pointer over the name of a collection. A tool-tip shows the path.

  • Team Collection is a way you can work together with other team members.
