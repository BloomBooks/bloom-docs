Page

A page is typically defined as one side of a sheet of something printed or written such as a book.

About pages in Bloom:

  • In the Edit tab, the right pane is called the Pages pane.

    • It shows a thumbnail for each page in the current book.

    • Different kinds of pages appear with different colored thumbnails.

    • Each page has a label that informs you about the kind of page that it is, or it shows a page number.

    • There are outside pages (front cover and back cover pages) and inside pages.

    • If you click a page, a small down arrow button appears.

  • Pages can have text boxes or image boxes, or both.

  • You can duplicate a page or use Copy and Paste.

  • Pages can be set to different page sizes.

Note

The page contents can look different in various electronic devices.
