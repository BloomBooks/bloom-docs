Inside pages

Inside pages are between the Front Cover and Back Cover pages.

They are mainly the book manuscript pages that you can add and have page numbers. But the Title Page and Credits Page are also considered inside pages because they are inside of the cover pages.

  • The Pages pane in the Edit tab distinguishes the cover pages from the inside pages with different colors.

  • The Publish tab distinguishes the cover pages from the inside pages so you have the option of printing the cover pages and inside pages on different paper.

Note

  • Page numbers in the published book appear after the Credits Page.

You can choose the page number style.
